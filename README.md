## 1. Character Concept

Before touching a pencil or prompt, **define who your character is.**

Ask yourself:

| Question | Mr. Red's Answer |
|---|---|
| What is their archetype? | The cold, calculating strategist |
| What do they value? | Discipline, power, execution |
| What's their vibe? | Corporate dark side — wealth without warmth |
| What makes them memorable? | Red accents in a world of black and shadow |

> 💡 **Rule:** One character = one dominant emotion. Mr. Red's is *controlled intensity*.

## 2. Visual Design Blueprint

Break the design into layers:

### 🧍 Body & Pose
- **Pose type:** Seated, reclined — power through *stillness*, not action
- **Body language:** Chin resting on hand → thinking, calculating
- **Silhouette:** Angular jawline, broad shoulders in a fitted suit
- Anime male proportions: head ≈ 1/8 of total height, elongated limbs

### 👁️ Face
- Sharp, narrow eyes (half-lidded = confidence/boredom)
- Dark, thick eyebrows — slight furrow
- Strong jaw, high cheekbones
- Accessories: **tinted red sunglasses** — mystery + status signal

### 💇 Hair
- Messy-textured black hair with **red highlight tips**
- Medium length, swept back with loose strands falling forward
- Conveys: someone powerful who doesn't care about being perfectly groomed

### 👔 Outfit
- Black suit jacket (slightly open, casual power)
- White dress shirt — unbuttoned at collar (controlled dishevelment)
- Dark tie, loosened
- Black luxury watch on left wrist
- Stud earring — subtle rebellion within a formal setting

## 3. Color Palette

```
Background city:  #0A0A1A  (near-black night)
Suit:             #1A1A1A  (deep black)
Shirt:            #F0EDE8  (warm off-white)
Hair base:        #0D0D0D  (black)
Hair highlights:  #CC0000  (deep red)
Phone:            #B22222  (crimson red)
Skin:             #C8956C  (warm tan)
Eye tint:         #8B0000  (dark red)
City lights:      #1A3A5C  (dark blue-grey)
Accent glow:      #FF2020  (hot red — used sparingly)
```

> ⚠️ **Red is your accent, not your base.** Use it on max 3–4 elements. The power of red comes from contrast with all the black.

If you're using **Stable Diffusion**, **Midjourney**, or **NovelAI**, here's the structured prompt pattern:

### ✅ Positive Prompt

```

black suit, white shirt, red-tinted sunglasses, black messy hair with red highlights,
holding red smartphone, luxury watch, moody lighting, nighttime city background,
office setting, red accent lighting, notebook on desk, laptop visible,

```

## 5. Drawing by Hand — Key Anatomy Tips

### Head Construction
```
Step 1: Draw an oval — flatten slightly at the bottom (chin)
Step 2: Draw a vertical center line + horizontal eye line (upper third)
Step 3: Eyes sit ON the eye line — space = 1 eye-width apart
Step 4: Nose = halfway between eye line and chin
Step 5: Mouth = 1/3 of the way from nose to chin
```

### Anime Eye Detail (for this character type)
```
- Outer corner slightly higher than inner corner (sharp, mature)
- Iris = almond shape, NOT round
- Heavy upper eyelid line
- Single thin lower lash line
- Minimal shine dots (2 max — this is a serious character, not sparkly)
- Add sunglasses OVER the eye structure
```

### Hands (the hardest part)
- Sketch as **basic geometric blocks** first (palm = trapezoid, fingers = cylinders)
- Holding a phone: 4 fingers curl under, thumb on side
- Don't skip the knuckle bumps — they add realism
- Wrist at slight angle when holding phone naturally

## 6. Lighting & Mood

Mr. Red uses **Rembrandt-style rim lighting** — dark everywhere except a glow from behind.

```
Light source 1: Soft red glow from the phone screen (front, low, warm)
Light source 2: City lights through window (back, blue-cool, creates rim)
Light source 3: Implied desk lamp (side, subtle fill)

Shadow rule: If a surface faces AWAY from all three sources → pure dark
Highlight rule: Only edges facing the city window get a thin blue-white rim
```

> This 3-light setup is what makes the character feel **cinematic** vs flat.

## 7. Props & Environment

Every prop must **reinforce character identity**:

| Prop | What It Says |
|---|---|
| Red smartphone | Active, connected, in control |
| Black luxury watch | Values precision, old money discipline |
| "Strategy Over Distraction" notebook | Anti-noise, focused mindset |
| "Discipline Today, Success Tomorrow" mug | Delayed gratification philosophy |
| "Focus. Plan. Execute. Repeat." poster | Character's literal life motto |
| Leather chair | Earned comfort, not gifted |
| City skyline at night | Empire-level ambition |

> 🎯 **Design tip:** Every single prop should be something the character would *choose* — not just filler. If you can't explain why it's there, remove it.

## 9. Common Mistakes to Avoid

```diff
- Making the red too bright — it kills the dark mood
- Forgetting shadows under the jaw and collar
- Drawing anime eyes too large for a mature male character
- Overloading with detail — let negative space breathe
- Making the suit look flat — add crease lines at elbows, collar, chest
- Ignoring background — even a blurred city silhouette anchors the scene
- Perfect symmetry — real faces (and anime characters) are slightly asymmetric
+ Use photo reference for the suit, pose, and hands
+ Keep the face relatively simple — expression is in the eyes only
+ Add a single strong detail (the red phone, the watch) and build around it
```

## 10. Final Checklist

Before calling your character done:

- [ ] Silhouette reads clearly at small size (thumbnail test)
- [ ] Color count is under 6 main tones
- [ ] Red appears in 3–4 places maximum
- [ ] Pose communicates the personality without text
- [ ] Background doesn't compete with the character
- [ ] Face has ONE clear emotion readable in < 2 seconds
- [ ] Props all have a reason to exist
- [ ] Lighting is consistent (all shadows on the same side)
